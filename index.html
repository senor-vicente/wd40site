Our Story

The West Denver Speech & Debate Club (WD-40) began as a single campus in 2017. Today, we cultivate winsome speaking, critical thinking, listening with discernment, and gracious, confident speakers.

As a member of Stoa National Speech and Debate, we serve privately educated students across the nation. Our forensics league embodies a culture of public discourse and intellectual inquiry, focused on building a world-class community of leaders.
