Our Story

The West Denver Speech & Debate Club (WD-40) began as a single campus in 2017. We cultivate winsome speaking, critical thinking, listening with discernment, and gracious, confident speakers.

We are a member of Stoa National Speech and Debate. Stoa’s forensics league embodies a culture of thought, public discourse, and intellectual inquiry, focusing on building a world-class community of leaders.
